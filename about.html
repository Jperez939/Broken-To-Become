"Broken to Become"
Designed, photographed, and edited by Joselyn Hernandez


 Designed to be a visual meditation on the beauty of breaking and becoming. This book centers on rebuilding from damage, using shards of glass as a source of beauty.

Starting from the beginning to the end it maintains a black and white visual system to show the opposites (breaking vs. becoming) and create an underlying connection to the Yin and Yang.
